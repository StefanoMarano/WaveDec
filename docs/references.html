Scientific publications related to WaveDec¶

How to cite us¶

If you use WaveDec in your work, please acknowledge it by citing the original work where the algorithm was first introduced:

Stefano Maranò, Christoph Reller, Hans-Andrea Loeliger, and Donat Fäh, “Seismic waves estimation and wavefield decomposition: application to ambient vibrations”, Geophys. J. Int. (2012) 191 (1): 175–188.

@ARTICLE{2012:MarRelLoeFae,
  author = {Stefano Maran{\`o} and Christoph Reller and Hans-Andrea Loeliger and Donat F\"{a}h},
  title = {Seismic Waves Estimation and Wavefield Decomposition: Application to Ambient Vibrations},
  journal = {Geophys. J. Int.},
  year = {2012},
  volume = {191},
  pages = {175--188},
  number = {1},
  month = {Oct.},
  doi = {10.1111/j.1365-246X.2012.05593.x}
}

For extensions to WaveDec and applications see the complete bibliography below.

Bibliography¶

The following publications are related to the development WaveDec and WaveDecActive:

Maranò_et_al_2017b

Stefano Maranò, Manuel Hobiger, Paolo Bergamo, and Donat Fäh, “Analysis of Rayleigh Waves with Circular Wavefront: a Maximum Likelihood Approach”, Geophys. J. Int., accepted for publication. doi:10.1093/gji/ggx225.


Maranò_et_al_2017a

Stefano Maranò, Manuel Hobiger, and Donat Fäh, “Retrieval of Rayleigh Wave Ellipticity from Ambient Vibration Recordings”, Geophys. J. Int. (2017), 209 (1): 334–352. Free access online.


Maranò_et_al_2014b

Stefano Maranò, Donat Fäh, and Yue M. Lu, “Sensor Placement for the Analysis of Seismic Surface Waves: Sources of Error, Design Criterion and Array Design Algorithms”, Geophys. J. Int. (2014) 197 (3): 1566–1581. Free access online, BibTex.


Maranò_et_al_2014

Stefano Maranò and Donat Fäh, “Processing of translational and rotational motions of surface waves: performance analysis and applications to single sensor and to array measurements”, Geophys. J. Int. (2014) 196 (1): 317–339. Free access online, BibTex.


Maranò_et_al_2012

Stefano Maranò, Christoph Reller, Hans-Andrea Loeliger, and Donat Fäh, “Seismic waves estimation and wavefield decomposition: application to ambient vibrations”, Geophys. J. Int. (2012) 191 (1): 175–188. Free access online, BibTex.


Maranò_et_al_2011

Stefano Maranò, Christoph Reller, Donat Fäh, and Hans-Andrea Loeliger, “Seismic waves estimation and wave field decomposition with factor graphs”, in Proc. IEEE Int. Conf. Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing, Prague, Czech Republic, May 2011, pp. 2748–-2751. doi:10.1109/ICASSP.2011.5947054.


Reller_et_al_2011

Christoph Reller, Hans-Andrea Loeliger, and Stefano Maranò, “Multi-sensor estimation and detection of phase-locked sinusoids”, in Proc. IEEE Int. Conf. Acoustics, Speech, and Signal Processing, Prague, Czech Republic, May 2011, pp. 3872–-3875. doi:10.1109/ICASSP.2011.5947197.
